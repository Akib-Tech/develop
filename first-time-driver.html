FIRST TIME DRIVER

COURSE DETAILS

For a full course description of the 4-Hour New Driver Drug and Alcohol Course.

At Boca Bay DUI And Traffic School know today’s young adults are busy with school, extracurricular activities, work, and studying, therefore with you in mind we introduce to you the online version of the Florida Drug and Alcohol Course. This program was designed with all the aforementioned concerns in mind, but also with the flexibility to complete the course at the student’s schedule.

Registration is easy, simply fill in the required information on the registration page, choose a password and answer 10 security questions. (The security questions will appear randomly during the course, and must be answered by you correctly in order to verify that you are the person who is taking this course.) ….and best of all you can begin as soon as you complete the registration process.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

The course is divided up into sections ranging in time from 15-40 minutes. You can log on & off as many times as you like and complete the course at your convenience. In order to receive credit for a section you must complete the entire section before logging off, if you do not want to continue. Then when you log on again it will take you to the next section. There is a 40 question “open book” test at the end. You must get 80% (32 questions) correct to get credit for the course. You are guaranteed to pass – Free retakes.

The Course fee is $40
