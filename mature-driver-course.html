MATURE DRIVER COURSE

OVER 55? RECEIVE AN INSURANCE DISCOUNT TODAY!

As a Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (DHSMV) approved provider, we are proud to offer the Coaching the Mature Driver Course. In accordance with Florida Statute 627.0652, if you are over 55 years of age, you’re eligible to earn a reduction on your car insurance premiums. By taking the course, you will be able to have a deduction on your rates, which can range from 5%-10% annually depending on your provider, and it will be valid for 3 years. After 3 years, you would be eligible to re-take the course to maintain your insurance rates discount.

NOTABLE INSURANCE PROVIDERS DISCOUNTS:

Geico: 10%

Allstate: 10%

Progressive: 5%

State Farm : 5%
