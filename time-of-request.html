  • POLICY:

    • Time off should be requested at least two weeks in advance of the requested day(s) off. Last minute Time-Off Request also follows the same procedure.

    • Fill in Time-Off Request with the following information; Date Leaving, Date Returning & Reason. *Time-Off Request Also Apply To Leaving The Office Early. AN EMAIL SHOULD ALSO BE SENT IF YOU ARE LEAVING THE OFFICE EARLY OR COMING-IN LATE.
